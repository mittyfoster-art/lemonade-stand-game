The game runs as a web app on a local network. There is nothing to install or download.

  • Make sure your device (phone, tablet, or laptop) is connected to the same WiFi network as the host computer running the game server.
  • Open any modern browser: Chrome, Safari, or Firefox all work great.
  • Navigate to http://192.168.33.247:4000/ and the game will load immediately.
Tip: If the page does not load, double-check your WiFi connection. The game will not work over mobile data since it runs on the local network.

When the game loads, you will see the home page with a "Join Game" form.

  1. Enter your name (this is what appears on the leaderboard).
  2. Enter the room code provided by the facilitator.
  3. Tap "Join Game" to enter the room.

Tap "Play Level" in the navigation (sidebar on desktop, bottom bar on mobile) to start playing.

Each level presents a unique business scenario set in the Cayman Islands. Read the story and hint carefully, as they contain clues about what strategies will be most profitable.

You make 3 decisions each level:

Price — $0.25 to $2.00 per cup

How much you charge customers. Higher prices mean more revenue per sale but may reduce demand.

Quality — 1 to 5 stars

Better ingredients attract more customers but increase your cost per cup.

Marketing — $0 to $30

Advertising spend. More marketing brings awareness, but the cost comes out of your budget.

Once you have set your decisions, tap "Open for Business!" to see your results. Then tap "Next Level" to continue.

Every player begins with a $500 budget. Your goal is to grow it by making smart business decisions.

  • Fixed costs: Each level deducts $20 for stand setup and permits.
  • Profit formula: Revenue from sales minus all costs (fixed + marketing + ingredients).
  • Game Over: If your budget falls below $20, you can no longer afford to open your stand.

Keep an eye on the budget indicator color:

$100+ Healthy $50 - $99 Caution Under $50 Danger

At certain milestone levels, you will receive a loan offer. This adds a real-world financial literacy element to the game.

  • Loan offer levels: 15, 20, 25, 30, 35, and 40.
  • You can Accept or Decline each offer.
  • If you accept, cash is deposited into your budget immediately.
  • You then repay a fixed amount each level until the loan is paid off.
  • You can only carry one active loan at a time.
Strategy: A well-timed loan can rescue a struggling business or give a thriving one the capital to invest in high-quality levels. Think carefully about whether the repayment schedule fits your plan.

How to Play In-game reference guide

This is a test session, so we want to exercise as many features as possible. Here is what to focus on:

  • Try different strategies — compare aggressive pricing versus conservative approaches to see how the simulation responds.
  • Accept at least one loan to test the loan system end-to-end, including repayments.
  • Play through at least 5 to 10 levels so you get meaningful results and can see trends on the profile charts.
  • Check the leaderboard to confirm other players' scores appear correctly.
  • Test both orientations — try portrait and landscape on your phone to catch any layout issues.
  • Report bugs or confusion to the facilitator. Anything that feels odd or unclear is valuable feedback.

A few things to be aware of during this local test session:

  • The leaderboard only shows players from your device since we are testing on a local network.
  • If the page feels stuck or unresponsive, try refreshing your browser. The game state is saved, so you will not lose progress.
  • The Awards page needs multiple players with completed levels before it fully populates. It may look empty at first.
Remember: This is an internal test. Do not worry about "breaking" anything. The more edge cases you find, the better the final product will be.
